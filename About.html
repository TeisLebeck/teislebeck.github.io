About

My name is Teis Lebeck and I work with data analysis in the field of privacy and data protection. Mainly, I analyze websites and apps for things like privacy violations, third-party tracking and data collection infrastructure. I have a background from Copenhagen Business School, where I graduated with a Cand.Merc.(kom) specialized in digital communication. I did my master thesis (in english) on third-party data collection on danish public websites, which you can find here.

Since then, I have worked in the Danish government with enforcing the ePrivacy-directive - the rules that dictate how websites and apps can use cookies and collect data about their users. Here, I have used different types of analysis methods insights to support legal investigations and policy development. However, all projects on this site have been made in my free time and have no relation to my work in government.

If you want to use some of my work, you are more than welcome to do so.
